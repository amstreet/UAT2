Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Timothy Ehrlich

Dr. Ehrlich is a board-certified physician who believes healthcare works best when patients have a real relationship with their doctor. With expertise spanning hair restoration surgery, metabolic health, and hormone optimization, he provides comprehensive care that addresses the whole picture — not just individual symptoms.

Unlike large telehealth platforms where you're just another number, Dr. Ehrlich gives every patient his personal contact information. He conducts regular check-ins, personally reviews all lab work, and is available when questions arise — even outside business hours.

His approach is simple: treat every patient the way he'd want to be treated. That means honest conversations, individualized treatment plans, and a physician who genuinely cares about your outcomes.
